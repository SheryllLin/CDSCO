REGULATORY WORKFLOW AUTOMATION DEMO USE CASES

Usage note:
These examples are for reviewer support and system demonstration only.
The tool should produce draft analysis, suggested prioritisation, potential duplicates, and draft reports.
It must not be treated as a final medical, legal, or regulatory decision-maker.

USE CASE 1: SAE-STYLE ADVERSE EVENT REVIEW
======================================================================

Primary Narrative / Submission Text
Patient Ms. Kavita Sharma, female, 45 years old, reported severe skin rash and breathing difficulty after taking DrugX for 3 days. She was admitted to City Care Hospital for observation. Case ID: SAE-101. Reporter: Dr. R. Mehta. The event improved after stopping the drug. Follow-up causality assessment is pending.

Previous Version Text
Patient reported skin rash after taking DrugX. Case ID: SAE-101. Follow-up review is pending.

Duplicate Candidate Documents
DOC-1::Patient Ms. Kavita Sharma, female, 45 years old, reported severe skin rash and breathing difficulty after taking DrugX for 3 days. She was admitted to City Care Hospital for observation.

DOC-2::Patient Kavita Sharma reported severe rash and breathing difficulty after DrugX and was admitted to City Care Hospital.

DOC-3::Patient reported mild nausea after DrugY and was treated at home.
